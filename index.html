This Weeks Featured Escape

With over 30 locations all over the world, Escape Hunt continue to impress us with a huge variety to their puzzles that we've yet to see anywhere else. So despite the rooms being the same at every brach, the quality and work that have gone into them will always have you wanting to go back for more!
