Why You Should Be Playing

Team Building

Are you part of a team at work or just want to feel closer to some friends? Escape rooms are a great way to develop your relationship and communication abilities with the people you play with.


You Only Need One Partner

We all know how much stronger those bonds are between family and partners. You don't need a huge group to solve an escape room and in a lot of cases that may even prove more challenging. Working with loved ones will bring you closer together and is sure to be full of surprises.

Explore Somewhere New Or Play In Comfort

If you think all escapes are in rooms, you're in for a surprise! A lot of companies now run outdoor escapes, setting up mysteries and puzzles all over the city for any keen explorers. You can also have the fun brought to you with escape games that can be set up in your own home. You could even hire an escape van and try getting out of that during your lunch break at work!


Fit For All Minds And Ages

Escape rooms are fit for almost all ages. For kid groups most companies will require an adult present but there are plenty of escape rooms fit for kids and will provide a wealth of challenges to develop any young mind.
